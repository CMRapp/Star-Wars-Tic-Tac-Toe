Project Notes

This project is a re-work of a project I created in C++ 2009 with Visual Studio and the Dark GDK as part of my final project when working toward my AS Degree. (It was considerably easier to create in JavaScript!). Unfortunately my computer that had the final suite of Star Wars themed games crashed and I ho longer have the executable files. I did, however, print out the code!

Game Play

Jedi Battle is simply Tic Tac Toe. The center grid is reminiscent of Luke's targeting computer in his X-wing when he blew up the Death Star. Simply click on any square to mark it. X = Yoda and O = Vader. The current player's turn is indicated at the top of the screen. The results of the game will appear after a winner or draw is declared.

I truly hope anyone viewing this project gets a smile!
